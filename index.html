About Me

I am currently a senior at The University of Iowa pursuing a major in Business Analytics & Information Systems through The Tippie College of Business as well as a minor in Sport and Recreation Management. I am a dedicated student and employee with excellent communication skills, a natural ability to lead, and a strong ability to think critically and problem-solve.

My education and professional experience has equipped me with both technical and interpersonal skills. Through my coursework at the University of Iowa, I have gained hands-on experience with tools such as Python, SQL, Excel, Oracle, and Power BI to apply techniques like data analysis, wrangling, mining, transformation, and dashboard creation. Professionally, my experience in operations, sales, and customer service has strengthened my problem-solving, collaboration, and communication skills.

I am currently seeking employment following my graduation in the spring. I look forward to connecting with like-minded professionals to explore potential employment opportunities.

Course Highlights

Computational Thinking

Introduction to algorithmic problem-solving logic with Python; use of data structures and programming constructs to manipulate data and solve business problems.

Database Management

Learned to design, implement, and use relational database systems. This course emphasized conceptual, logical, and physical data modeling as well as hands-on skills with Structured Query Language (SQL).

Data Mining

Introduction to predictive analytics methods motivated by problems in operations, marketing, finance, and healthcare; data mining techniques, including classification, regression, and clustering.

Data Wrangling

Utilize Jupyter Notebook programming to collect, process, and manipulate data. Apply methods for descriptive and visual analytics to derive insights that can aid business decision making.

Sports Analytics for Decision Makers

This course explored how data is used to analyze player performance, improve scouting and recruitment, enhance fan engagement, and optimize strategic decision-making.
