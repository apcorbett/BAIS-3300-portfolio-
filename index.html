About Me

I am currently a senior at The University of Iowa pursuing a major in Business Analytics & Information Systems through The Tippie College of Business as well as a Minor in Sports and Recreation Management. My education and professional experience have equipped me with both technical and interpersonal skills. Professionally, my experience in operations, sales, and customer service has strengthened my problem-solving, collaboration, and communication skills.

I am currently seeking employment following my graduation in the spring. I look forward to connecting with like-minded professionals to explore potential employment opportunities.
